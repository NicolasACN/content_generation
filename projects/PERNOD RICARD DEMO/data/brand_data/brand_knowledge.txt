Beefeater, a premium gin produced in the heart of London, stands as the world’s most awarded gin. It has been part of the Pernod Ricard group since 2005 and maintains its production using the original 1863 recipe by James Borough. This includes a unique 24-hour steeping process of nine balanced botanicals. Beefeater embodies the Spirit of London, combining 200 years of distilling heritage with a modern, energetic urban vibe suitable for today’s tastes, including a range of naturally flavoured gins.
